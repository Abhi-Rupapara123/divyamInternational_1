LT Panels

There are several types of electrical panels based on their functionality. They are as follows:

1. Power Control Centre (PCC)

Centralized control for better performance and safety.

2. Motor Control Centre (MCC)

The heart of your motor control system, designed for high performance.

3. Power Distribution Panel (PDB)

Reliable and safe power distribution for any facility.

4. Automatic Power Factor Correction Panels

Automatic, efficient, and intelligent power factor management.

5. Synchronizing Panels

Synchronize and control multiple power sources effortlessly.

6. Bus Ducts (Aluminum/Copper Busbar)

Robust, flexible, and efficient power distribution.

HT Panels

1. 11kV VCB Pane:

Precision-engineered for superior protection and performance.

2. APFC Panel:

Optimizing power factor for cost efficiency and system longevity.

3. Soft Starter Panel:

Smooth startup and protection for motors, extending equipment life.

4. Ring Main Unit (RMU):

Efficient and reliable distribution for modern utilities.
